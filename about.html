What's BIHAC

Bihak Center is an online and offline platform that works to boost self- development particularily for young people. Bihak center is a fruit of the convinction that a big number of people are not aware of and/or have not demosntrated their capacity because they lack a skill, support , or an opportunity to showcase the potential hidden within them.

About BIHAC CENTER

Our Motivation

We believe God has put in each of us the potential to live a good life. We want to help every person to discover and showcase that potential.

Our Mission

To help as many young people as possible to unleash their full potential to impact their environment positively.

Our Vision

A world in which every person has the appropriate skills and platforms to develop their full potential and improve their lives, change their communities and develop their countries.
